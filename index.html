About Me

I'm a full stack web developer and so much more...

Hi, my name is Lynde. It's nice to meet you. I am a full stack web developer, located in Gresham, Oregon. I became interested in programming several years ago when a co-worker of mine developed a really cool program that screened for and corrected human errors in our work. Unfortunately his idea wasn't well received by our IT department, but for the first time I saw the power behind programming and was fascinated. I left that job and for the last 8 years I have been a stay at home mom. When it was time for me to consider returning to work I dreaded the idea of doing what I was doing before. Don't get me wrong, there were many reasons why I loved doing what I had done previously, but the idea of starting over again felt demoralizing. That being said, starting a new career from scratch felt impossible. Balancing a family didn't leave me with much time for myself, but I couldn't get the idea of web development out of my head. After lots of research, I applied for and received a grant to help fund some technology training and I haven't looked back since. Learning to program hasn't been easy, and I still have a lot to learn, but I love it. It's challenging, it's rewarding, and it's limitless.

Outside of web development, I am a mom to three awesome kids. I am married to my best friend, and I have a dog named Drexler. I love to travel and practice my Spanish. I am an avid camper and have never had a summer without at least a few camping trips. I love to be on or in the water and enjoy paddle boarding immensely. I am very connected to my community, and volunteer in many capacaties when and where I can. Get in touch if you'd like to chat more. I'd love to talk!
